ABOUT US

    Welcome to our restaurant!. Our goal is to provide our customers with an exceptional dining experience that is both memorable and satisfying. Our chefs use only the freshest and highest quality ingredients to create our menu. From classic favorites to innovative dishes, our menu offers something for everyone. We believe that great food should be enjoyed in a warm and welcoming atmosphere, which is why we take pride in providing a friendly and attentive service to our customers. We understand the importance of community and family, which is why our restaurant is designed to be a place where people can come together to enjoy a great meal and make lasting memories. At our restaurant, we are committed to sustainability and responsible sourcing. We are dedicated to providing a truly exceptional dining experience to each and every one of our customers. Whether you are a regular or a first-time visitor, we look forward to serving you and sharing our passion for food with you. Our services are dining, catering, and buffet orders. Thank you for choosing our restaurant, and we hope to see you soon!

Menu

BIRIYANI

Our biryanis come in a range of flavors and styles, from classic chicken and lamb to vegetarian and seafood options.

NASI GORENG

Our Nasi Goreng is a classic Indonesian fried rice dish made with fragrant jasmine rice, mixed vegetables, and a savory blend of spices and sauces.

FRIED RICE

Our fried rice come in a range of flavors and styles, from classic chicken and lamb to vegetarian and seafood options.

KOTHTHU

Our Koththu is a perfect blend of textures and flavors, with crispy roti and savory fillings.

CURRY

Choose from a variety of curries, ranging from mild and creamy to spicy and aromatic, and discover your new favorite dish.

PAROTA

Indulge in the authentic taste of South Indian cuisine with our delicious and freshly made Parota.

CHAPATHI

Made with high-quality ingredients and cooked to perfection, our chapathis are soft and chewy.

TEA

From classic black tea to aromatic herbal blends, our teas are the perfect addition to any meal.
